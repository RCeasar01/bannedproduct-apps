Privacy Policy

FitnessAI — BannedProduct Media Inc.

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BannedProduct Media Inc. (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the FitnessAI mobile application (“App”). We are committed to your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Account email address (required for sign-in and cloud sync)
  • Camera and video footage captured during exercise sessions — processed on-device for form analysis; video is not uploaded to or stored on our servers
  • Apple Health data: steps, heart rate, and calories burned — read-only, with your permission
  • Workout logs, exercise history, and performance metrics
  • Device identifiers for crash reporting and analytics

Camera footage is processed entirely on-device and is never uploaded to our servers. You may revoke Health access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain the App, sync your data across devices, process subscriptions, respond to support requests, and improve the App through aggregated, anonymized analytics. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database for account and workout data sync
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Apple HealthKit — Read-only access to fitness data, with your permission

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transit (HTTPS/TLS) and Supabase’s built-in security controls. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion at any time.

Children’s Privacy

This App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@bannedproductmedia.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by updating the “Last updated” date above.
