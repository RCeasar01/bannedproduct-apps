Privacy Policy

GridDown — BannedProduct Media Inc.

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BannedProduct Media Inc. collects, uses, and protects information when you use the GridDown mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account email address — required only if you use the Community Alerts feature
  • Alert location — shared with other app users only when you actively post a community alert
  • Alert content you choose to post publicly within the app

All guide content, checklists, and reference material work fully offline with no account required. Account creation and location sharing are only needed for the Community Alerts feature.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain the App and respond to support requests. We do not sell your personal information.

Location Data

Location is shared with other GridDown users only when you actively post a community alert. The app does not track your location passively. You control when and what you share.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database powering the Community Alerts feature

Your Rights

Contact support@bannedproductmedia.com to access, correct, or delete your data. We respond within 30 days.
