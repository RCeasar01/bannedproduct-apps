Privacy Policy

HolyBook — BannedProduct Media Inc.

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BannedProduct Media Inc. collects, uses, and protects information when you use the HolyBook mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account email address — optional; only required if you enable cross-device sync
  • Reading progress and bookmarks — stored locally; synced to cloud only when an account is active
  • Location — accessed in real time to calculate prayer times; not stored, logged, or transmitted
  • App preferences and display settings

HolyBook works fully offline for Quran reading. No account is required unless you want to sync bookmarks and progress across multiple devices.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain the App, sync your data, and respond to support requests. We do not sell your personal information.

Location Data

Location is used solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. It is accessed in real time and is not stored, logged, or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

  • alquran.cloud — Arabic Quran text and audio content API
  • Supabase — Optional cloud sync for reading progress and bookmarks

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted transit (HTTPS/TLS) and Supabase’s built-in security controls.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact support@bannedproductmedia.com. We respond within 30 days.
