Privacy Policy

RangeLog — BannedProduct Media Inc.

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BannedProduct Media Inc. collects, uses, and protects information when you use the RangeLog mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account email address (required for cloud sync)
  • Range session data: date, location name, round counts, drill results, and notes
  • Firearm inventory: make, model, caliber, and user-assigned notes (serial numbers are not required fields)
  • Ammunition inventory and usage logs
  • User-initiated CSV export files (sent directly to your device; not transmitted to our servers)

Firearm serial numbers are never required and should not be entered. All inventory data is stored on-device first and only synced to the cloud if you have an account and cloud sync enabled. CSV exports are user-initiated and go directly to your device’s file system.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain the App, sync your data, respond to support requests, and improve the App. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database for account and sync data

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security including encrypted transit (HTTPS/TLS) and Supabase’s built-in security controls.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact support@bannedproductmedia.com. We respond within 30 days.
