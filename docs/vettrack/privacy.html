Privacy Policy

VetTrack — BannedProduct Media Inc.

Last updated: June 2026

⚠ VA Benefits Disclaimer Compensation calculations in VetTrack are estimates only and do not constitute official VA determinations. Always verify your benefit information directly with the VA or an accredited Veterans Service Organization (VSO).

This Privacy Policy explains how BannedProduct Media Inc. collects, uses, and protects information when you use the VetTrack mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account email address (required for cloud sync feature)
  • VA disability ratings and service-connected conditions you enter manually
  • Appointment dates and healthcare scheduling notes
  • Estimated compensation figures calculated from VA rating tables
  • Document vault files (DD-214, award letters, ID cards) — stored locally; cloud backup is opt-in only
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) — used only to lock/unlock the app; biometric data is handled by your device’s Secure Enclave and is never transmitted

Biometric data is processed entirely by iOS/Android Secure Enclave and never leaves your device. Document vault files remain on-device by default. Serial numbers, SSNs, and financial account numbers are not required fields and should not be entered.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain the App, sync your data, respond to support requests, and improve the App. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database for account and optional data sync

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security including encrypted transit (HTTPS/TLS) and Supabase’s built-in security controls.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact support@bannedproductmedia.com. We respond within 30 days.
