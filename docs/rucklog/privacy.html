Privacy Policy

RuckLog — BannedProduct Media Inc.

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BannedProduct Media Inc. collects, uses, and protects information when you use the RuckLog mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account email address (required for cloud sync)
  • GPS location data during active ruck sessions only — stored locally and optionally synced to Supabase
  • Ruck weight, distance, duration, pace, and elevation data
  • Gear configuration and loadout notes
  • Device timezone for accurate session timestamps

Session data is stored locally first. Cloud sync is opt-in and requires an account. You can export or delete your session data at any time from within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain the App, sync your data, respond to support requests, and improve the App. We do not sell your personal information.

Location Data

GPS location is accessed only during an active ruck session. Background location is used only while a session is in progress — tracking stops completely when you end the session. You can use RuckLog in manual-entry mode without enabling GPS.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database for account and session sync

Your Rights

Contact support@bannedproductmedia.com to access, correct, or delete your data. We respond within 30 days.
